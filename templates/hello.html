Intro

The Demon Slayer series, also known as "Kimetsu no Yaiba," is a Japanese manga and anime series set in the Taish≈ç era of Japan. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his younger sister Nezuko is transformed into a demon. Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to help Nezuko regain her human form and to fight against demons, including the powerful demon lord Muzan Kibutsuji and his twelve most powerful minions, the Twelve Kizuki.

Tanjiro's journey begins with his training under Giyu Tomioka, the Water Hashira, and Sakonji Urokodaki, who teaches him the "Water Breathing" style. He later meets other Demon Slayers, including Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira, and together they face various demons and battles. Along the way, Tanjiro discovers a mysterious Breathing Style known as "Hinokami Kagura" and continues his quest to help Nezuko and defeat the demons
